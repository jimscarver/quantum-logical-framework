**Spacetime and Matter Emergence**  
A demonstration of how space, time, and matter emerge from logical twist histories.

**Summary:** This document demonstrates how space, time, and matter emerge from simple logical twist histories. Physical entities such as the electron, neutrino, photon, and even the hydrogen atom are represented as strings of eight basic twists. By balancing spatial and local free actions within these strings, spacetime and the properties of matter naturally arise. All entanglement is treated as internal to the history itself.

**Examples**

1. **Electron**  
   ```python
   from spacetime_dynamics import SpacetimeGenerator
   gen = SpacetimeGenerator("^>v<^+/-")
   print(gen)
   ```
   **Output:**  
   Twist History: ^>v<^+/-  
   Length: 8 twists  
   Spatial: 4, Local: 4, Balance: 0  
   Interpretation: Stable particle-like
